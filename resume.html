Jim Dodge is a New York City based Comedian and Commercial Actor who resides in Hoboken, NJ. He has performed in all the major NYC comedy clubs and venues, and also spent several years doing road work across Middle America and the South. He was a Comedy Central Laugh Riots NYC finalist and has appeared on NBC’s “Jane’s New York”, “Late Night with Seth Myers”, and Comedy Central’s “The Daily Show.” He has been featured in several local and national commercials for companies such as McDonalds, Bank of America, and Lowes among others.
